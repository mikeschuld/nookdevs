                     Instructions
Hangman is a word guessing game,
where you are given 6 chances to reveal a word that is hidden behind a black unit.

You guess by clicking on letters, if the word contains a letter you pressed, then the all appropriate places containing the letter are revealed to you and the count of attempts do NOT lower.
However, if you press a letter that the word does not contain, then given attempts lower by 1 chance. If you reach zero - you lose.

Please note: ALL of the words that this game contains have been chosen and formatted one by one. ALL of them have been taken from the commonly used english word list, thus the game's difficulty is balanced making it enjoyable.
Hope you like it !
